# Corporate and Financial Databases

This page provides an overview of key corporate and financial databases available to members of the University of Mannheim, including access information, content descriptions, and support resources.

## Overview: Where to Find Which Data

| Data Type | Databases |
|----------------------------------|------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------|
| Prices | LSEG Workspace, Aktienführer-Archiv |
| Analyst forecasts | I/B/E/S (via LSEG Workspace) |
| Mutual funds | CRSP Mutual Funds, Institutional (13f) Holdings, Mutual Fund Holdings |
| Accounting data | Orbis, COMPUSTAT North America, COMPUSTAT Global, LSEG Workspace |
| Banks and financial service | Bank Focus, SNL Unlimited (S&P Global) |
| M&A and ownership structure | Aktienführer data archive, Zephyr, SDC Worldwide M&A |
| Executive information | Orbis, Aktienführer data archive, Dun & Bradstreet Hoovers, COMPUSTAT Execucomp |

## Database Descriptions and Access

### Wharton Research Data Services (WRDS)

Many financial databases are accessible via the web-based WRDS interface.

- [Access and university specific information](https://dbis.ur.de/UBMAN/resources/8646)
- [WRDS Website](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/)

### Aktienführer Data Archive

- **Contents:** Historic German financial market and corporate data (1870–2018), including standardized reports on corporations traded at German stock exchanges. Reports include information on head offices, management, operations, investments, shareholder structure, and income statements.
- **Available history:** 1956–2016 (Database), 1870–2016 (digitized full-text PDFs)
- **Access:** [Free access](https://digi.bib.uni-mannheim.de/aktienf%C3%BChrer/data/index.php) within the University of Mannheim IP range

### Bank Focus (Bureau van Dijk and Moody's Analytics)

- **Contents:** Comprehensive database of 44,000 banks worldwide, with detailed, standardized reports and ratios. Data sourced from annual reports, information providers, and regulatory sources.
- **Help:** [Bank Focus user guide](https://help.bvdinfo.com/mergedProjects/131_EN/Home.htm)
- **Access:** [Bank Focus](https://bankfocus.bvdinfo.com/ip) (Bureau van Dijk interface), or via [WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### COMPUSTAT Execucomp (Standard & Poor's)

- **Contents:** Executive compensation data from annual proxy statements (DEF14A SEC form), including salary, bonus, options, stock awards, incentive plans, pensions, and other compensation.
- **Help:** Documentation available via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### COMPUSTAT Global (Standard & Poor's)

- **Contents:** Financial and market data for publicly traded companies in over 80 countries, covering more than 90% of global market capitalization. Includes hundreds of data items and up to 12 years of annual history.
- **Help:** Documentation available via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### COMPUSTAT North America (Standard & Poor's)

- **Contents:** U.S. and Canadian company fundamentals and market information for over 30,000 companies, including income statements, balance sheets, cash flows, indices, and more.
- **Help:** Documentation available via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### CRSP (Center for Research in Security Prices)

- **Contents:** Security prices, returns, and volume data for NYSE, AMEX, and Nasdaq markets. Includes stock indices, portfolio data, treasury bond and risk-free rates, and mutual fund databases.
- **Help:** Data description and definition guide via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### Dun & Bradstreet Hoovers

- **Contents:** Over 220 million business records from 30,000 sources in 190 countries, including company data, sector, employees, risk classes, and enriched online and signal information.
- **Access:** [Dun & Bradstreet Hoovers](https://dbis.ur.de/UBMAN/resources/105992)

### I/B/E/S

- **Contents:** Consensus and detailed forecasts from security analysts, including earnings per share, revenue, cash flow, growth projections, and stock recommendations.
- **Help:** Documentation available via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### Institutional (13f) Holdings

- **Contents:** Institutional Common Stock Holdings and Transactions as reported on SEC Form 13F, covering managers with $100 million+ in assets.
- **Help:** Documentation available via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### LSEG Workspace (including Datastream and Worldscope, formerly Refinitiv Workspace/EIKON)

- **Contents:** Comprehensive company and market data, including financial indicators, stock market, bonds, money and foreign exchange markets, and commodities. Data is available for download.
- **Help:**
  - [LSEG Finance Essentials](https://www.lseg.com/en/training/learning-centre/certification/lsegfe-certification)
  - [LSEG Workspace tutorials and courses](https://www.lseg.com/en/training/learning-centre/)
  - [Excel Add-In tutorial](https://www.lseg.com/content/lseg/en_us/training/learning-centre/learning-centre-catalogue/video-details.html?id=getting-started-with-refinitiv-workspace-excel-add-in&playlistId=1_6rg1cjpw)
  - [Datastream datatype description (PDF)](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/media/Lehrstuehle/bwl/Maug/Database_info/Datastream_dataypes.pdf)
- **Access:**
  - Access limited to University of Mannheim faculty and students.
  - [Request a personal account](https://www.bib.uni-mannheim.de/medien/zugang-zum-lseg-workspace/) for regular use (faculty and doctoral students: semester; students: three months).
  - Short-term remote access via two virtual terminals managed through the ILIAS group [Finance Databases](https://ilias.uni-mannheim.de/goto.php?target=crs_1227320&client_id=ILIAS).
  - For access questions, contact the [subject librarian for business administration](https://www.bib.uni-mannheim.de/en/resources/subject-specific-research/). For ILIAS group questions, contact [Stefan Scharnowski](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/theissen/team/postdocs-und-doktoranden/stefan-scharnowski/).

### Mutual Fund Holdings

- **Contents:** Security holding information for all registered mutual funds reporting to the SEC, plus 3,000 global funds. Includes NYSE/AMEX/NASDAQ, Toronto, and Montreal common stocks.
- **Help:** Documentation available via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### Orbis (Bureau van Dijk)

- **Contents:** Information on over 275 million companies worldwide, with a focus on private companies. Enables research, analysis, and evaluation of companies, with standardized data for cross-border comparison.
- **Help:** [Orbis user guide](https://help.bvdinfo.com/mergedProjects/68_EN/Home.htm)
- **Access:** [Orbis](http://dbis.uni-regensburg.de/warpto.php?bib_id=ubman&color=8&titel_id=12630&url=https%3A%2F%2Forbis4.bvdinfo.com%2Fip) (Bureau van Dijk interface)
- **Extended Access:** For bulk downloads and historical updates, extended access is available to professors, academic staff, and supervised student theses. Contact [orbis@uni-mannheim.de](mailto:orbis@uni-mannheim.de) to reserve access.

### SDC Worldwide Mergers, Acquisitions & Alliances (Thomson Financial)

- **Contents:** Data on worldwide mergers, acquisitions, alliances, and stock repurchases. Covers approximately 672,000 global M&As from 1985 to present (U.S. data since 1979), and stock repurchase programs from 1994.
- **Help:** Documentation available via WRDS.
- **Access:** [via WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### SNL Unlimited (S&P Global)

- **Contents:** Financial data, news, and analyses for over 6,500 public and 50,000 private companies in banking (worldwide), insurance (USA, Canada, Europe), and financial services (USA, Canada).
- **Help:** After login, click the "Help" button in the database header.
- **Access:** Restricted to University of Mannheim members; personal registration with university email required via [S&P Global registration page](https://platform.spgi.spglobal.com/).

### Zephyr (Bureau van Dijk)

- **Contents:** Comprehensive deal database for M&A, IPO, private equity, and venture capital deals and rumors. Contains information on 1.6 million deals and rumors, with over 100,000 added annually.
- **Help:** [Zephyr user guide](https://help.bvdinfo.com/mergedProjects/83_EN/Home.htm)
- **Access:** [Zephyr](https://zephyr.bvdinfo.com/ip) (Bureau van Dijk interface)
