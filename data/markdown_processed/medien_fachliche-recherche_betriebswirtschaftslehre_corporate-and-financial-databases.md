# Corporate and Financial Databases

## Where to Find Which Data

| Data Type | Databases |
| --- | --- |
| Prices | LSEG Workspace, Aktienführer-Archiv, CRSP |
| Analyst forecasts | I/B/E/S |
| Mutual funds | CRSP Mutual Funds, Institutional (13f) Holdings, Mutual Fund Holdings |
| Accounting data | Orbis, COMPUSTAT North America, COMPUSTAT Global, LSEG Workspace |
| Banks and financial service industry | Bank Focus, S&P Capital IQ Pro |
| M&A and ownership structure | Aktienführer data archive, Zephyr, SDC Worldwide M&A |
| Executive information | Orbis, Aktienführer data archive, Dun & Bradstreet Hoovers, COMPUSTAT Execucomp |

## The Databases in Detail

### Wharton Research Data Services (WRDS)

Many financial databases are available via the web-based interface of [WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/). For access and university-specific information, see [DBIS entry](https://dbis.ur.de/UBMAN/resources/8646).

### Aktienführer Data Archive

The [Aktienführer data archive](https://digi.bib.uni-mannheim.de/aktienf%C3%BChrer/data/index.php) is a key source for historic German financial market and corporate data, published between 1870 and 2018. It contains standardized reports from German and foreign corporations traded at German stock exchanges, including information on head offices, management, operations, investments, shareholder structure, and income statements.

- **Available history:** 1956–2016 (Database), 1870–2016 (digitized full-text PDFs)
- **Access:** Free within the IP-range of the University of Mannheim

### Bank Focus (Bureau van Dijk and Moody's Analytics)

[Bank Focus](https://bankfocus.bvdinfo.com/ip) is a comprehensive database of banks worldwide, combining content from Bureau van Dijk and Moody's Investor Service. It contains detailed, standardized reports and ratios for 44,000 banks (29,000 US and 15,000 Non-US). Templates are regularly updated to reflect the latest accounting and regulatory disclosures.

- **Help:** [Bank Focus user guide](https://help.bvdinfo.com/mergedProjects/131_EN/Home.htm)
- **Access:** Bureau van Dijk interface or via [WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### COMPUSTAT Execucomp (Standard & Poor's)

Execucomp provides executive compensation data collected from annual proxy (DEF14A SEC form), including salary, bonus, options, stock awards, incentive plans, pensions, and other compensation items.

- **Help:** Documentation and manuals via WRDS
- **Access:** [WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### COMPUSTAT Global (Standard & Poor's)

COMPUSTAT Global offers financial and market data for publicly traded companies in over 80 countries, covering more than 90% of global market capitalization. It includes hundreds of data items, ratios, and up to 12 years of annual history.

- **Help:** Documentation and manuals via WRDS
- **Access:** [WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### COMPUSTAT North America (Standard & Poor's)

COMPUSTAT North America provides U.S. and Canadian fundamental and market information on over 30,000 companies, including more than 300 annual and 100 quarterly data items.

- **Help:** Documentation and manuals via WRDS
- **Access:** [WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### CRSP (Center for Research in Security Prices)

CRSP maintains a comprehensive collection of security prices, returns, and volume data for NYSE, AMEX, and Nasdaq markets. Additional files include stock indices, portfolio data, treasury bond, and risk-free rates.

- **Help:** Data description and definition guide via WRDS
- **Access:** [WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### Dun & Bradstreet Hoovers

[D&B Hoovers](https://dbis.ur.de/UBMAN/resources/105992) aggregates over 220 million business records from 30,000 sources in 190 countries, including company data, online sources, and signal information, updated daily.

### I/B/E/S

I/B/E/S provides consensus and detailed forecasts from security analysts, including earnings per share, revenue, cash flow, growth projections, and stock recommendations.

- **Help:** Documentation and manuals via WRDS
- **Access:** [WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### Institutional (13f) Holdings

Thomson/Refinitiv Institutional Holdings (13F) Database provides institutional common stock holdings and transactions as reported on Form 13F filed with the SEC.

- **Help:** Documentation and manuals via WRDS
- **Access:** [WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### LSEG Workspace (including Datastream and Worldscope)

LSEG Workspace is a comprehensive source for company and market data, including financial indicators, stock market, bonds, money and foreign exchange markets, and commodities. Most data is available for download.

- **Help:**
  - [LSEG Finance Essentials](https://www.lseg.com/en/training/learning-centre/certification/lsegfe-certification)
  - [LSEG Workspace tutorials](https://www.lseg.com/en/training/learning-centre/)
  - [Excel Add-In tutorial](https://www.lseg.com/content/lseg/en_us/training/learning-centre/learning-centre-catalogue/video-details.html?id=getting-started-with-refinitiv-workspace-excel-add-in&playlistId=1_6rg1cjpw)
  - [Datastream datatype description (PDF)](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/media/Lehrstuehle/bwl/Maug/Database_info/Datastream_dataypes.pdf)
- **Access:**
  - Limited to University of Mannheim faculty and students
  - Personal accounts for frequent users (faculty, doctoral students: semester; students: 3 months)
  - Short-term remote access via two virtual terminals managed through the [ILIAS group Finance Databases](https://ilias.uni-mannheim.de/goto.php?target=crs_1227320&client_id=ILIAS)
  - For access questions, contact the [subject librarian for business administration](https://www.bib.uni-mannheim.de/en/resources/subject-specific-research/). For ILIAS group questions, contact [Stefan Scharnowski](https://www.bwl.uni-mannheim.de/theissen/team/postdocs-und-doktoranden/stefan-scharnowski/).

### Mutual Fund Holdings

Thomson/Refinitiv Mutual Fund Holdings database provides security holding information for all registered mutual funds reporting to the SEC, plus 3,000 global funds.

- **Help:** Documentation and manuals via WRDS
- **Access:** [WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### Orbis (Bureau van Dijk)

Orbis contains information on over 275 million companies worldwide, focusing on private company information. It enables research on individual companies or groups, search by profile, and company analysis. All data is standardized for cross-border comparisons.

- **Help:** [Orbis user guide](https://help.bvdinfo.com/mergedProjects/68_EN/Home.htm)
- **Access:** [Orbis](http://dbis.uni-regensburg.de/warpto.php?bib_id=ubman&color=8&titel_id=12630&url=https%3A%2F%2Forbis4.bvdinfo.com%2Fip) (via Bureau van Dijk interface)
- **Extended Access:** For bulk downloads and historical updates, extended access is available for professors, academic staff, and supervised student theses. Contact [orbis@uni-mannheim.de](mailto:orbis@uni-mannheim.de) to reserve access.

### SDC Worldwide Mergers, Acquisitions & Alliances (Thomson Financial)

SDC Worldwide M&A provides data on worldwide mergers, acquisitions, alliances, and stock repurchases, covering approximately 672,000 global M&As from 1985 to present (U.S. data since 1979).

- **Help:** Documentation and manuals via WRDS
- **Access:** [WRDS](https://wrds-www.wharton.upenn.edu/login/) (registration required)

### S&P Capital IQ Pro

S&P Capital IQ Pro offers financial data, news, and analyses for over 6,500 public and 50,000 private companies in banking, insurance, and financial services.

- **Help:** After login, click the "Help" button in the database header
- **Access:** Restricted to University of Mannheim members; registration with university email via [S&P Capital IQ Pro registration page](https://platform.spgi.spglobal.com/)

### Zephyr (Bureau van Dijk)

[Zephyr](https://zephyr.bvdinfo.com/ip) is a comprehensive database for M&A, IPO, private equity, and venture capital deals and rumors, with information on 1.6 million deals and over 100,000 added annually.

- **Help:** [Zephyr user guide](https://help.bvdinfo.com/mergedProjects/83_EN/Home.htm)
- **Access:** Bureau van Dijk interface
